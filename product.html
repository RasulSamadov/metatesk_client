Pillar 02

AI Teacher

Not a chatbot. Not a quiz engine. Metatesk's AI Teacher is an intelligent educational companion that adapts to each student's pace, answers questions in real-time within the VR environment, and evolves toward full AI teacher avatars with voice-in/voice-out interaction.

Every student learns differently — that's why Metatesk offers four distinct teaching personas. Students choose the teacher that fits how they think and learn best.

Real-time Voice Interaction Ask questions naturally inside the VR world. The AI responds contextually with voice and visual cues.
Adaptive Pacing The AI adjusts explanation depth, speed, and style to match each individual student's comprehension level.

Professor Ada

Patient Guide

Step-by-step explanations with comprehension checks. Never rushes. Builds understanding layer by layer until it clicks.

Dr. Felix

Energetic Motivator

Real-world examples, high energy, strong analogies. Makes connections between subjects and life — turns dry topics into discoveries.

Professor Maia

Thought Provoker

Socratic method — asks strategic questions instead of giving answers directly. Builds critical thinking and deep reasoning skills.

Dr. Rex

Efficient Expert

Concise, direct, no fluff. Maximizes knowledge density per minute. Perfect for advanced students who want speed and depth.

Pillar 03

Multiplayer Classrooms

Education is inherently social. Metatesk's multiplayer infrastructure brings up to 30 students together inside a shared virtual environment — with live voice communication, collaborative experiments, and teacher-led group sessions happening in real time.

Students learn together, interact with simulations as a class, and build understanding through shared experience — the same social dynamic as a physical classroom, but with the power of an immersive virtual world.

30-Student Capacity A full class of 30 students can share a single virtual environment simultaneously — teacher and students together.
Live Voice Chat Real-time spatial voice communication — students and teachers talk naturally as if physically in the same room.
Collaborative Experiments Students work together on virtual experiments, lab simulations, and interactive challenges — learning through doing.
Teacher-Led Sessions Educators control the classroom environment — guiding students, presenting content, and managing the session from within VR.

Pillar 04

LMS Platform

What started as an internal tool to manage Metatesk's own deployments grew into a full-featured, institutional-grade learning management system. Schools, education networks, and governments use it to deploy, manage, and scale their immersive learning operations.

Built for national scale — not just a classroom. The Metatesk LMS is the infrastructure layer that makes it possible to bring immersive education to an entire country's school system.

Institutional Deployment Deploy Metatesk across an entire school, network of schools, or national education system with centralized management.
Progress Analytics Track individual student progress, class-wide performance, and institutional outcomes — with actionable data for educators and administrators.
Multi-School Management Manage multiple schools or institutions from a single dashboard — user accounts, content, sessions, and analytics in one place.
National Scale Ready Designed for B2G deployment — scalable infrastructure that supports thousands of schools and millions of students.
